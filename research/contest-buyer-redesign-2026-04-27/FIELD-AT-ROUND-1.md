## Convergences (the field's unanimous reads)

- **All 5 want the H1 visible.** Currently it's `sr-only`. Unanimous structural fix.
- **All 5 rewrote "Browse Products" CTA to a buyer-task verb** ("Find My Compliance Documents", "Get My Compliance Documents", "Find Your State's Documents"). Easiest, highest-probability win.
- **4 of 5 proposed a `/compliance-deadline-by-state` (or similar) hub page** — independent validation, not groupthink.
- **All 5 increased blog → product CTA density** (current: 1 link per 1500-word post; proposed: 3+ placements per post, with multiple internal links).
- **All 5 hit the same diagnostic data point** — position 4.16 / 0.11% CTR on the EEOC blog as the central illustration of the problem.

---

## The One Real Divergence

**Contestant 1 says abandon informational queries.** Bet: the 1,833 impressions/post are unreachable because AI Overviews are eating them; the effort should go to transactional queries that AI Overviews don't absorb.

**Contestants 2 and 3 say earn citation inside AI Overviews.** Bet: brands cited within an AI Overview answer get 35% more clicks; the existing informational traffic is partially recoverable through structured-data and content-format work.

These are not logically incompatible — you could pursue transactional targeting AND optimize for AI Overview citation on existing informational content. But they rest on different bets about where the leverage is. **Hold yours, pivot, or articulate the both/and.**
