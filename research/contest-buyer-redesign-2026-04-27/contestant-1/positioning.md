## Voice Comparison (what changed from Round 1)

**Round 1 (Realist/Credentialist, research-resource tone):**
> "aicompliancedocuments.com serves small and mid-size business owners who use AI in hiring, HR, lending, insurance, or consumer-facing decisions, and who have just discovered — from counsel, a vendor audit, a regulatory notice, or a Google search at 11pm — that they are legally exposed..."

**Round 2 (Calm Authority, compliance-store tone):**
> "...is the place you go when you have a state AI compliance deadline and you need the documents — not the explanation."

**What changed:** The Round 1 version narrates the buyer's journey (how they got here, what they feel). The Round 2 version states what the site is and what it offers. Less backstory, more identity. The buyer doesn't need us to explain their situation back to them — they need us to confirm we have what they came for.
