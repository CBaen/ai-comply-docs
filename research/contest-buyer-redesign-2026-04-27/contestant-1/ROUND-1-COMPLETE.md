# Round 1 Complete — Contestant 1

**Concept:** The Zero-Click Pivot — Stop targeting informational queries that AI Overviews consume, and start targeting transactional queries that only a product page can answer. The strategy has three components: (1) rewrite above-the-fold homepage copy to speak to deadline-anxious business owners rather than AI compliance researchers, with real law names, real dates, and real penalty math; (2) restructure product pages to lead with the buyer's exposure situation before the document list and buy button; (3) inject a mandatory `<BlogProductCTA>` component at two anchored positions in every blog post covering a law with an available product — directly addressing the structural leakage where 1,833-impression blog posts produce near-zero product page clicks. The research gate confirmed that the site's top competitors for buyer-intent queries (TXAIMS for Texas, CO-AIMS for Colorado, TrustArc for enterprise) all occupy different market positions — none of them own the "multi-state, instant-download, statute-sourced, $49–$697" niche the site already holds. The winning move is not to be louder; it is to be clearer about who the site is for and what happens if they don't act.
