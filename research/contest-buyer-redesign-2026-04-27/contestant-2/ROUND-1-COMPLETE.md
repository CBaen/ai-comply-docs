# ROUND-1-COMPLETE — Contestant 2

## Concept

The site has earned position 4.5 for "ai compliance packages" — a buyer query — and generates zero clicks from it because the title tag looks like a research portal. The approach I've taken is a signal-matching redesign: metadata and homepage copy must match what buyers type and expect to find, not what researchers type and find useful. The six deliverables address this at every layer — from the `<title>` tag that appears in the SERP to the 3-touchpoint CTA pattern in blog posts to the FAQ schema that positions product pages (not just blog posts) to appear in AI Overview citations. I chose not to invent a new positioning frame — the site's existing voice (Pragmatic Realist + Precise Credentialist) is already the right answer; it just needs to appear in the places buyers look first.
