## Reasoning

That insight should now drive a full brand identity, not just a title tag rewrite.

What I'm committing harder to: **opinionated minimalism.** Every change I propose in Round 2 is the minimum that ships the new identity. Not sparse for its own sake — sharp because every element earns its place. The visual direction will be tight, not ornate. The IA will be flatter, not deeper. The voice will be short, not clever. The new brand identity is "a hardware store that stocks exactly what the law requires and knows which aisle it's in" — not a research library, not a SaaS platform, not a law firm. Confident. Transactional. Exact.
