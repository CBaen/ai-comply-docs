# ROUND 1 COMPLETE — Contestant 3

## Concept Summary

This entry is built around one diagnosis: the site already has everything a buyer needs, but nothing that routes them from awareness to purchase. The positioning change is not a brand overhaul — it is a series of precise interventions at the exact moments when a buyer transitions from "reading about the law" to "I need to do something about this." The homepage gets a visible H1 and deadline-anchored copy that names the buyer's situation in the first sentence. Product page titles gain deadline urgency that changes SERP behavior at position 11 (Colorado). Blog posts get 3-5 inline product links plus a CTA card placed immediately after the penalties section — the moment of maximum buyer motivation, which is currently where the posts go quiet. One new page (`/compliance-deadline-tracker`) is proposed as a structured reference that earns AI Overview citations and routes undifferentiated buyers to the right state product. Every penalty figure and every effective date in the copy is verified against a .gov primary source fetched in this session — no training-data legal facts anywhere in the deliverables.
