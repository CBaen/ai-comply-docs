## Key Strategic Insight From Research

The competition is all enterprise SaaS or law firms. The site's actual advantage — instant download, statute-built, $49-$697 — is never stated as the primary value proposition. Instead, the homepage talks about the catalog ("Templates for Every State AI Law") when it should be talking to the buyer's specific situation ("Colorado's deadline is June 30. You use AI in hiring. Here's what you need.").

The blog posts are excellent researcher content that sends 0 buyers to product pages. The link density is one link per 1,500 words — insufficient for the urgency moment when a reader realizes they're covered. The fix is structural: every law-specific blog post should have 3+ product-intent links placed at the moment of recognition, not buried in closing sections.
