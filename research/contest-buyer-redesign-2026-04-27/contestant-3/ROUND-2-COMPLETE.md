# ROUND 2 COMPLETE — Contestant 3

## Concept Summary

The Round 2 identity is a compliance store, not a research library. The brand voice is short, declarative, product-forward: "Your state passed an AI law. Here are the documents." Every surface — homepage, product page, blog CTA, ad creative — is rewritten around that frame. The visual direction is opinionated minimalism: five colors with strict semantic rules (amber for urgency only, green for statute verification only), one typeface, no carousel, no lifestyle imagery that generic-ifies, no screen-reader-only H1. The IA demotes the blog from primary nav to a Resources dropdown, moves the penalty section above the document list on product pages (motivation before solution), and builds the homepage section order around the buyer's decision journey rather than the founder's explanation of the product. The `/compliance-deadline-tracker` hub earns its place as the first item under Resources — it's the compliance store's "what do you need this for?" index, and the structured-data format positions it for AI Overview citation. The ad creative follows the same voice: three words, a price, and a deadline. Nothing more is needed for a buyer who already knows they have a problem.
