### High Confidence (agents converged with independent evidence)

All three agents independently agreed on these findings:

1. **SEO will not produce revenue in 30 days.** New domain sandbox is 6-12 months. Not debatable.
2. **Active outreach is the only path.** Passive channels (SEO, AEO) are medium-term plays, not 30-day plays.
3. **SHRM chapter speaking is the single strongest play.** Exact buyer persona, zero cost, educational format, Colorado deadline creates urgency. Triple convergence.
4. **Questionnaire-before-payment is the highest-leverage conversion fix.** 5-6 steps of data entry before seeing a price/checkout button. All three agents flagged this.
5. **Founder identity is a conversion requirement.** Anonymous compliance products at $299-$997 do not convert cold traffic. Triple convergence — the council's clearest signal.

### Recommended Approach: 30-Day Sprint

**Week 1 (Foundation):**
- Add Cameron's full name and real photo to the About page and homepage (already has photo, needs name)
- Restructure 3-4 blog posts for AEO (lead with the answer, add specific numbers, include publication dates) — this is a "plant seeds" action, not a 30-day revenue play
- Pitch Mile High SHRM and 2 other Colorado SHRM chapters for a 20-minute educational presentation on SB205

**Week 2-3 (Active Outreach):**
- Begin genuine participation in r/humanresources, r/compliance, r/smallbusiness — answer questions about AI compliance with genuine expertise, site in profile bio only
- Cold email 10-20 Colorado HR managers per day using state business registry and Built In Colorado as data sources — lead with the regulation, not the product
- Follow up on SHRM chapter responses; prepare presentation if booked

**Week 4 (Compound):**
- Product Hunt launch for backlink + credibility signal (2-3 hours)
- Continue Reddit/Quora participation
- Continue cold email outreach
- Measure: any Stripe payment notifications?

**NOT recommended:**
- AppSumo — 120-day commitment, audience mismatch, revenue share too high for a product that hasn't validated its core buyer yet
- LinkedIn — firm boundary, do not pursue
- Paid advertising — no budget

### Conversion Fixes to Ship This Week

1. **Founder identity** — full name on About page and homepage. This is the #1 conversion requirement per triple convergence.
2. **Questionnaire friction reduction** — consider showing pricing/checkout summary earlier, or allowing "Quick Purchase" without full questionnaire for buyers who know what they want
3. **Email capture on quiz page** — the quiz generates personalized recommendations but captures zero contact information for follow-up

### Alternatives

**AppSumo** — The iubenda precedent is compelling (compliance product → GDPR deadline → AppSumo launch → traction). But the audience mismatch (startup founders vs. HR compliance officers) and the 120-day lock-in make this a second-order play, not a first-revenue play. Consider after first 5-10 organic sales prove the product converts.

**AEO-first strategy** — Restructuring content for Perplexity citations could produce traffic in 2-4 weeks. But AEO produces citations, not purchases. The conversion blockers (anonymous founder, questionnaire friction) gate revenue regardless of traffic source.

### Disagreements

**Colorado SB205 exemption scope:** The DA says businesses under 50 employees are exempt. The ER says the exemption is conditional (training on own data). This matters for addressable market size. Needs resolution from the enacted statute text — browser Claude should verify.

**Feasibility of 30-day revenue:** CA scored 7/10, DA challenged to 5/10. The spread reflects genuine uncertainty — the site is technically ready but has zero market validation. The DA's Failure Severity of 9/10 (Cameron's personal situation) adds weight to acting fast and tolerating imperfection.

### Filtered Out

- Generic "build an email list" advice — no list exists, no traffic exists, building from zero takes months
- Influencer partnerships — no budget, no network
- LinkedIn anything — destructive boundary
- Conference sponsorships — no budget
- SEO content production at scale — already has 12 strong posts, more content won't rank faster

### Risks

1. **No human has tested the actual documents.** If the first customer finds errors, the trust damage is severe. (DA finding, validated by all)
2. **SHRM chapter scheduling may miss the 30-day window.** Monthly meetings may already have full agendas. The DA challenged the ER's Integration Effort from 5/10 down to 3/10 on this basis.
3. **Cold email deliverability from a new sender.** Without an established sending domain, emails may land in spam. Resend (the current email provider) handles transactional email but may not be ideal for cold outreach.
4. **The compliance document template market has free competitors.** Termly, Osano, and others provide free privacy/compliance generators. Cameron's differentiation (statute-specific, citation-verified) requires the buyer to already know they need something better than free.
