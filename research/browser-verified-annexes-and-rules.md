## NYC LL144 Rules — 6 RCNY §§ 5-300 to 5-304 (CONFIRMED from DCWP PDF)

### Bias Audit (§ 5-301):
- Must be within 1 year of use
- Calculate selection rate AND impact ratio for each category
- Categories: Sex (Male/Female), Race/Ethnicity (7 EEO-1 categories), Intersectional (all Sex × Race/Ethnicity combinations)
- Impact Ratio = category selection rate ÷ most selected category rate
- Can exclude categories <2% with justification
- Parallel requirements for scoring tools (scoring rate instead of selection rate)

### Published Results (§ 5-303):
- Must be on employment section of website, clear and conspicuous
- Must include: audit date, data source/explanation, unknown category count, selection/scoring rates and impact ratios for ALL categories, distribution date
- Must remain posted 6 months after last AEDT use
- Can use hyperlink to separate page

### Notice (§ 5-304):
- Must include instructions for requesting alternative selection process or accommodation
- 3 delivery methods: website (10 business days before), job posting, or mail/email (10 business days before)
